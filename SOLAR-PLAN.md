### Why WhatsApp Instead of SMS

1. **Legal:** WhatsApp risk is substantially lower than SMS (IP-based messaging, not carrier SMS). TCPA has generally NOT been applied to WhatsApp for personalized messages referencing a prior relationship — but the law is not fully settled. SMS to the 281 leads = up to $411K exposure. WhatsApp voice notes, kept personalized and honoring opt-outs immediately, are low-risk but not zero-risk.
2. **Response rate:** Voice notes in Spanish convert 5-10x higher than phone calls for Latino audiences. Johan's Colombian accent is his unfair advantage.
3. **Speed:** Instant delivery vs mail delays or voicemail wait.
4. **Cost:** Zero. No carrier fees, no bulk SMS costs, no postage.

### The Continuous Nurture Principle

Every lead stays in active nurture until:
1. ✅ They close (become a customer)
2. 🛑 They explicitly opt out
3. ⏰ 12 months of zero response across all channels
