# Postcard Design — Cold Lead Re-Engagement

For the 243 non-HOT leads (Tier 2 WARM + Tier 3 CONTACT).
Design in Canva Free. Print at Staples or VistaPrint. Standard 4x6 postcard.

---

## FRONT (Eye-catching side)

**Layout:** Bold headline on a clean background. One strong visual element.

**Background:** White or light blue (#f0f4f8). Clean, not busy.

**Headline (large, bold):**

```
Your NJ Electric Rate
Just Went Up Again.
```

**Subheadline (smaller, below):**

```
You asked about solar a while back.
The savings are even better now.
```

**Visual:** Simple illustration or icon of a house with solar panels + a downward arrow on a utility bill. Or just a sun icon. Keep it clean — not stock-photo busy.

---

## Printing & Mailing

**Option A — DIY (cheapest):**
- Design in Canva Free (4x6 postcard template)
- Print at Staples: ~$0.25-0.40/card for color on cardstock
- Buy stamps: $0.56 each (postcard rate)
- Total per card: ~$0.80-1.00
- Total for 243 cards: ~$195-243
- Handwrite names for higher open/read rate (or print labels)

**Option B — Print service:**
- VistaPrint or PostcardMania
- Bulk pricing around $0.30-0.50/card including postage for 250+
- Total: ~$75-125
- They handle printing and mailing
- Less personal but faster

**Timing:**
- Mail during week 2 of Johan's outreach sprint
- Expect responses to trickle in over 2-3 weeks
- Track via Bitly click count + form submissions with utm_source=postcard

## Compliance Notes

- Physical mail has NO consent requirements (unlike SMS/calls)
- Must not be deceptive (FTC Act Section 5)
- Must include return/sender address
- Must include compensation disclosure
- Do NOT make it look like an official government notice or urgent legal document
- No Do Not Mail list exists at federal or NJ level
