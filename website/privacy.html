Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our consultation form, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number
  • Interest Information: Monthly electric bill range
  • Language Preference: Which version of our site you submitted from (English or Spanish), so we can contact you in your preferred language
  • Consent Record: Whether you agreed to our contact terms, the consent language you agreed to, and the date and time of your submission

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Contact you about solar options if you request a consultation
  • Provide you with information about solar programs available in your area
  • Improve our website and services

3. How We Share Your Information

NJ Solar Consultant is an independent solar consultant and receives compensation from partner installers when homeowners go solar. We may share your information with:

  • Solar Broker Partners: If you request a consultation, we may share your contact details and property information with our solar broker partner so they can provide you with options. The broker will obtain your separate consent before any additional parties contact you.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party tools (Google Sheets) to process and store form submissions

We do not sell your personal information to unrelated third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Text Message (SMS) and Phone Consent

By checking the consent box and submitting our form, you provide prior express written consent to receive calls and text messages (including by autodialer) from NJ Solar Consultant at the phone number you provided.

  • You may receive up to 10 text messages over 14 days.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase.

5. Data Storage and Security

Form submissions are transmitted to our secure data collection systems (Google Sheets via encrypted connection). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as needed to provide you with solar consultation services, or until you request deletion. Consent records are retained for a minimum of 5 years for legal compliance purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of text messages by replying STOP
  • Opt out of emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.

8. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies for advertising purposes. We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our site. If we add tracking tools (such as a Meta Pixel for advertising), this policy will be updated before those tools are activated.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
