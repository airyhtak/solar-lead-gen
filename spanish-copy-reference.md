# Spanish Copy Reference — NJ Solar Consultant

Neutral Latin American Spanish, **"usted"** form throughout. Matches Colombian Spanish norms (Johan is Colombian) and works across all Latino audiences in NJ (Dominican, Puerto Rican, Ecuadorian, Mexican).

## Tone Rules
- **Usted**, not tú — formal but warm
- No regional slang (no Mexican "órale", no Caribbean "ñapa", no Argentine "che")
- Clear, professional, neighborly
- "Vecino" (neighbor) — warmest neutral option
- Colombian Spanish is known for being clear and well-structured — lean into that

## Language Assignment Logic

Every new lead enters the CRM with a `Language` field set to `en` or `es`. This routes them to the correct funnel touchpoints automatically:

- **Website form (EN page):** hidden field `language=en` → English funnel
- **Website form (ES page):** hidden field `language=es` → Spanish funnel
- **Postcard QR (EN variant):** URL → welcome-back.html (English)
- **Postcard QR (ES variant):** URL → welcome-back-es.html (Spanish)
- **Cold calls from 243-lead list:** use pre-populated Language field (based on surname analysis)
- **Door knocking:** Johan asks "¿Habla español?" and logs in CRM before leaving the porch
- **Nextdoor/FB DMs:** language of the thread determines which copy to use
